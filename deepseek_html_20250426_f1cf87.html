About Sports for Hope

Founded by student-athletes committed to social change, we're redefining sports education through community reinvestment

More Than Just Sports Lessons

Sports for Hope was founded in 2023 by Bryan Suprien and Aaron Haldar, two high school athletes who recognized the growing disparity in sports accessibility for lower-income families. What began as a simple idea to share their athletic expertise has grown into a nonprofit movement that combines quality sports instruction with meaningful community impact.

Our unique model: For every paid lesson we provide, we're able to fund equipment, coaching, and facilities for children who otherwise couldn't participate in organized sports. It's a win-win that elevates entire communities through athletic opportunity.

We specialize in introductory and intermediate level training for tennis, soccer, cricket, and volleyball - focusing on fundamental skills, sportsmanship, and personal development. Our coaches are not just skilled athletes, but mentors committed to making sports accessible to all.

Based in Hillsborough, NJ, we serve families throughout Somerset County with flexible scheduling and locations. Additional travel fees may apply for lessons beyond a 10-mile radius from our home base.

Our Sports Programs

Quality instruction in four popular sports, designed for beginners and intermediate players aged 8-18

🎾

Tennis

Develop proper stroke technique, footwork, and match strategy through our progressive tennis curriculum. Perfect for players looking to learn fundamentals or improve their competitive game.

⚽

Soccer

Master ball control, passing, shooting, and game awareness through dynamic drills and small-sided games. Our program emphasizes technical skills and soccer IQ development.

🏏

Cricket

Learn proper batting, bowling, and fielding techniques from a national-level player. Our cricket program introduces this growing sport to new players while developing competitive skills.

🏐

Volleyball

From serving and passing to attacking and blocking, our volleyball instruction covers all aspects of the game with a focus on proper mechanics and team play.
